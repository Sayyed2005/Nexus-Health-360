üß© System Architecture (Modular & Incremental)

Step 1: Core Digital Foundation

Patient registration & profiles

Appointment scheduling

Billing & invoices


Why it matters:

Step 2: Operational Intelligence Layer

Purpose: Solve real hospital bottlenecks.

Modules:

Bed availability & occupancy tracking

Staff allocation & shift planning

Workflow automation (admissions, discharge, transfers)


Why it matters:
Most inefficiencies in hospitals are operational. This layer directly improves resource utilization and patient flow, delivering immediate value.


---

Step 3: Predictive Analytics & AI Layer

Purpose: Add intelligence using trusted data.

Modules:

Resource demand forecasting

Patient inflow prediction

Anomaly detection & alerts


Why it matters:
AI is introduced only after clean and sufficient data exists, ensuring reliable predictions instead of assumptions.

Modules:

Why it matters:

Purpose: Enable production-grade deployment.

Modules:
